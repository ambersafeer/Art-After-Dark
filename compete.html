COMPETITIONS ARE NOW OPEN FOR 2024!

Are you a current high school or college student artist based in New Jersey? Apply now to compete and be featured at the 5th annual Art After Dark festival!

Art After Dark recognizes NJ-based students for outstanding artistic achievement. This competition amplifies young voices, provides a platform for artists, and provides them with the resources needed to succeed in their future careers.

About the Application

Art After Dark hosts competitions within four artistic disciplines: Digital Art, Film, Illustration, and Mixed Media. As the festival expands and we gain more support, we hope to incorporate more categories and prizes for winners. Currently, we partner with the Zimmerli Art Museum to help bring student art to the public eye after the festival concludes.

Each artist may submit up to 3 artworks per category, but note that each artist may only be chosen as a winner once to help bring support to other artists. The application requests that you provide your name, contact information, high quality photos of your artwork, its name, and a description relating to your artistic process and inspiration for the piece.

Awards and Benefits

  • Museum Exhibition

    • All winners of the competition will be able to showcase their winning artowrks in a collective exhibition at the Zimmerli Art Museum.

  • Cash Prizes

    • Winners will receive monetary awards ranging from $100 to $500.

  • Public Display

    • All entries (winners or not) will be displayed at the Art After Dark festival, given that the artist is able to attend and bring their artwork.

  • Network of Support

    • All participating artists will be given access to like-minded peers, professionals in their field, and developmental workshops to hone their skills.

  • Future Scholarships

    • Winning artists will be given access to apply for scholarships to help with collegiate funding.
